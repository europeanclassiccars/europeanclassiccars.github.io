CHARMING 1926 SINGER 10/26,2 SEAT OPEN TOURER.
Here is a charming and very rare Singer 10/26 2 seat open tourer from 1926.Imported from the Sidney area of Australia in 2000.
Confirmed as being built in November 1926,it has a straight 4 cylinder,1308cc ohv engine,coupled to a 3 speed,plus reverse gearbox and rod brakes to the rear.
4 owners since repatriation to the UK.
An older restoration,she starts,drives and stops but has been off the road for a decade or so.So some recommissioning will be required.Good UK history and some period pictures from Australia.

Any inspection welcome.
