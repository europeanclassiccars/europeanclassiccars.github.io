1996 FIAT BARCHETTA 1.8 ROADSTER,with factory hard top.
Just 45k miles.
On offer here is my own,lovely Fiat Barchetta,with factory hard top.This is a superb example with lovely,straight,rust free bodywork and structure,excellent interior,the desirable factory hard top and a very modest 45k miles covered.

Originally sold new in Germany,it found it's way to the UK via Holland and was UK registered 16/01/2019.Having fancied a Barchetta for a number of years,I purchased this example late in 2018,I've enjoyed the car over the last year and in all honesty would very happily keep her! It has terrific handling and good performance from the 1800cc,twin cam engine,a tight,rattle free ride and of course is great fun with the hood down!
 It had a full cambelt service last year,including the timing variator,so has plenty of miles & years left in that.It uses no oil,has a lovely,slick gearchange and as mentioned is cosmetically,bodily and structurally in superb condition.I looked at 3 other examples before purchasing this car and none were as clean and tidy underneath as this example,or included the factory hard top.
The keen eye'd will have spotted the rear badge is not original,I still have the original badge,however it is faded and I decided it was not up to the standard of the rest of the car.

The car comes with the full German owners pack,spare key (brown master),has excellent tyres and will be sold with a fresh mot.

Dare I say,the thinking persons MX5,overall a superb example of this terrific little roadster.It has to be one of the best in the UK.
