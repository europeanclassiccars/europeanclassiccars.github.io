BLANCHE,A 1973 CITROEN HY VAN,CATERING VEHICLE.
On offer here is Blanche,a fabulous Citroen H Van,converted into a catering vehicle.
It comes with a fully fitted kitchen and a pizza oven all ready to go for this years festivals and events.

In a very nice colour combination of  Rouge Delage and Old English White and with no graphics applied,so ready for you to put your own mark on it.It retains it's 1900cc petrol engine and is in full running order.
A very common vehicle to use as a catering van,due to it's character & charming looks no doubt,significant space and flat floor.The H Van had a very long production life,starting in 1947 and running for a remarkable 34 years.The classic,corrugated industrial look panels make them stand out from the usual classic vans.
Our example is in nice order,no corrosion issues to be worried about and in good mechanical order,it starts easily and drives well,albeit quite slowly! The kitchen is kitted out and ready to use and comes with the pizza oven as shown in pictures.Please take a close look at the pictues to see that it fits your requirements.
