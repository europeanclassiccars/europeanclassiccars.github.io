LOCATING & INSPECTING CLASSIC CARS IN EUROPE
The internet is a wonderful thing,most classic car enthusiasts find themselves browsing the web looking at all those classics on offer across the water.
Over the years we have gained experience,not only in inspecting cars on mainland Europe,but also in importing and registering the cars back here in the UK.
If you have found yourself a vehicle over the water that you would potentially like to import but don't want to take the risk of buying blind and don't have the time to view or the experience with importing and registering cars here,we are happy to save you the hassle and carry out inspections,arrange importation and complete UK registration.
Please email or call to discuss terms.

We are also always looking for top quality classic's of all marques but especially classic Lancia,Alfa Romeo,Fiat,Ferrari,Porsche.Part restored or restoration projects also considered.
If you are trying to sell your car contact us and we may be able to help.
