EARLY 1980'S REVELL ROMANY TOURING BIKE 22.5" (57CM) FRAME .
Here is a really lovely early 80's Revell Romany Touring bike that has had just one lady owner from new!
In a really nice shade of metallic blue the frame is a quality frame made from Reynolds 531 lightweight tubing,this extending to the forks as well as frame.It has Campagnolo front & rear dropouts also.Some of these Revell frames were built by Mercian Cycles which gives an idea of the quality of build the brand was looking for.
Attached to the frame are the quality,period components you'd hope for on a touring bike,Suntour gears,SR chainset,bars & stem,Brooks leather saddle,Weinmann cantilever brakes,Weinmann rims with Maillard hubs.
Gold ESGE mudgaurds are fitted along with a period rear carry rack.

The bike has just been completely stripped,serviced and rebuilt.This including regreasing of headset and rear axle,front axle and bottom bracket were very smooth.All other components have been cleaned and serviced as required.New outer gear and brake cables were fitted along with new bar tape and amberwall tyres.

As a result of this work the bike now rides and looks superb,a few paint marks have been touched in but these are minor and overall the cosmetic condition is super. The bike rides extremely well,the gears work as they should and the bike rides tight and rattle free.

A lovely 80's,quality touring bike that has been loved by it's first and only owner and that is now ready for new adventures and travels with a new keeper.
